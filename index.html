F.A.Q

Is Scely.ia an insurance company?
Scely is a licensed agency by the Insurance Regulatory Authority (IRA) to operate in Kenya. We act as a link between the customer and the insurance company. The insurance products we offer are underwritten by reputable insurance companies in Kenya.
Can I choose my preferred insurance company?
YES. We consider various factors and recommend the best insurance company, but the final decision ultimately belongs to the client.
Is it more expensive when buying insurance from Scely.ia than directly from insurance companies?
NO. We compare offers from different insurance companies and recommend the best option, thus saving you time and money. We also negotiate for the best terms possible, so in most cases, our prices are actually lower than buying directly from the insurance company.
